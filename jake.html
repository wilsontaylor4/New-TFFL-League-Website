Jake Wolfer

Over his 9 seasons in the TFFL, Jake Wolfer has shown flashes of brilliance, claiming two division titles. While a championship has not been in the cards yet, he has consistently been a tough opponent with a 48.3% career win percentage. His 2022 season was particularly strong, ending with a final standing of 2nd place.
