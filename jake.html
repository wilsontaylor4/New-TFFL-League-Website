Jake Wolfer

Jake is a good friend of Taylor and was a groomsman in his wedding. His wife, Brynn, was one of Taylor's wife Shanna's best friends growing up. Taylor and Jake became very close friends once they were all living in Riverside and did everything together for many years until Taylor and Shanna moved to Washington. A fan of the Cincinnati Bengals, Jake has consistently been a strong contender, winning two division titles in 2018 and 2020. He came close to winning a championship in 2022, where he finished in second place. His solid overall win percentage reflects his competitive nature.
