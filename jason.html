Jason Zamora

Jason, Stephen's son, has been an active owner for 10 seasons and also worked at The Home Depot for a while. A fan of the Los Angeles Chargers, he reached the pinnacle of the league in 2022, when he won his first TFFL championship. Jason has also secured two division titles, highlighting his ability to navigate the regular season successfully.
