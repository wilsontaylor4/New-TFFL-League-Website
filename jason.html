Jason Zamora

A student of fantasy football, Jason Zamora has proven he has the skills to compete in the league and secured his first championship in 2022 with his team, Henry Given Sunday. Jason, along with his younger brother Stephen, has been in the league since the 2014 season. His team names have always been well-thought-out, including a tribute to one of the most iconic fantasy football characters, "The Commish's Curse."
