Mitch Sennewald

Mitch Sennewald has enjoyed a long and successful career in the TFFL. Over 11 seasons, he has accumulated a strong 55.5% win percentage and two championships (2015 and 2017). Mitch has also secured two division titles, showcasing his consistent performance in the regular season. He is a formidable opponent and a staple of the league's history.
