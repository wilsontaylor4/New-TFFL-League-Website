Mitch Sennewald

Mitch, Taylor's brother-in-law, has been a fierce competitor for 11 seasons. He relocated to Portland, OR after competing in the league for several years. He is one of only three owners with multiple championships, claiming his titles in 2015 and 2017. He has historically been one of the league's biggest trash talkers and is known for forming intense rivalries and constantly switching his team names. His performance has largely justified it. His strong career is also highlighted by two division titles.
