Riley Kuhl

Riley, Taylor's best friend since childhood, has competed for all 12 seasons of the league. A fan of the Seattle Seahawks, he has had some strong years, including two runner-up finishes in the championship. Riley has competed from Pueblo, CO for the entire existence of the league, but has been able to attend a couple of drafts in person over the years. Despite not yet having a championship, his consistent performance has kept him in the top half of the league in terms of total record and win percentage.
