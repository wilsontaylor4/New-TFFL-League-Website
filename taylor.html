Taylor Wilson

As "The Commish" and creator of the TFFL, Taylor put the league together with a mix of friends, family, and co-workers. A passionate fan of the Kansas City Chiefs, he has been a competitor for all 12 seasons and holds the top career total win percentage. Taylor relocated to Vancouver, WA during the league's existence, making him one of the owners who has had a major relocation. Taylor is known for building elite regular-season teams, but he has often fallen victim to the "Commissioner's Curse," winning only one of the four championships he's competed in, and frequently having abrupt playoff exits despite elite regular seasons.
