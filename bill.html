Bill & Kathy Kuhl

Bill & Kathy Kuhl are two-time TFFL champions, winning titles in 2018 and 2021. They have been a part of the league for 11 seasons and have a career win percentage of 48.7%. Their consistent performance and championship success make them a respected team in the league.
