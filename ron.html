Ron Wilson

Ron, Taylor and Taryn's father, has been a competitor for all 12 seasons. Also a fan of the Kansas City Chiefs, he is a childhood friend of Bill and the TFFL's most successful owner in terms of championships, having claimed the title three times. His ability to perform in the playoffs has cemented his legacy as the most decorated champion. Ron now competes from Prescott, AZ, making him one of the owners who has had a major relocation during the league's existence.
